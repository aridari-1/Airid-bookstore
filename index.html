"Every book starts with a question I can't stop thinking about."

Airid is the author of four debut novels spanning mystery thriller, supernatural dark fantasy, dark romance, and psychological science fiction. Each book is set in a different world, written in a different register, and built around a character who refuses to be reduced to a single idea.

The Last Frequency began with a silent radio tower. The Blood of Forgotten Gods began with what cities bury beneath themselves. Kill Switch began with a woman who reads every page of a contract before she signs it. The Mirror Protocol began with the question: what if grief technology worked perfectly — and that was the problem?
